Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

MatchCV ("we", "our", "us") is a mobile app that helps you analyze resumes, match job descriptions, generate cover letters, and get AI career coaching. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

1. Information we collect

  • Resume files you upload (PDF, DOCX, TXT)
  • Job descriptions you paste for matching
  • Chat messages sent to MatchCV Coach
  • Usage data such as feature limits and app interactions (stored on your device)
  • Email (optional, if you create an account in future versions)

2. How we use your data

We use your data only to provide MatchCV features:

  • Resume analysis and ATS scoring
  • Job match and keyword suggestions
  • Cover letter generation
  • AI career coaching
  • Processing subscriptions (via Google Play / App Store and RevenueCat)

We do not sell your personal data.

3. AI processing

Resume text and chat content may be sent to third-party AI providers (e.g. Groq, OpenAI) to generate analysis and responses. Data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS. Do not upload information you do not want processed by AI.

4. Data storage & retention

  • Some data is stored locally on your device (preferences, usage counters)
  • Uploaded files are processed on our servers and not kept longer than needed for the request
  • You may request deletion by contacting us

5. Third-party services

  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • Google Play / Apple App Store — payments
  • AI providers — resume and chat processing
  • Hosting providers — backend API

6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your data, and to object to certain processing. Contact us to exercise these rights.

7. Children

MatchCV is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top will change when we do.
