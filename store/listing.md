## Full description
73% of resumes never reach a human — killed by ATS filters.

MatchCV scores your CV, finds missing keywords, and shows how well you match any job — before you apply.

**Features:**
- Upload and analyze your resume (PDF/DOCX/TXT)
- ATS compatibility score with detailed breakdown
- Match your resume against any job description
- Generate personalized cover letters (Formal, Friendly, Confident)
- MatchCV Coach for interview prep and resume tips
- Premium: unlimited scans, full keyword lists & AI tools
