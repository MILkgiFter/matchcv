## App language in Play Console

Add these **languages** under Store settings → Manage translations:
`en-US`, `pt-BR`, `es-ES`, `pl-PL`, `de-DE`, `fr-FR`, `tr-TR`

The in-app language picker (Profile → Language) controls UI + AI responses independently of device locale.
